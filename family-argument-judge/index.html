Family Argument Judge

Family debates can last for years over subjects no historian would dare record: shoes at the door, holiday timing, leftovers, remote controls, and who said what in 2014.

Who Is Wrong turns harmless family arguments into funny mock-court verdicts made for laughs and group chats.

Family Cases Worth Judging

  • Who gets the final say over the holiday schedule?
  • Is it rude to move someone's shoes from the front door?
  • Who is wrong in a sibling argument about borrowed clothes?
  • Does "because I said so" count as evidence?

Use With Care

This is for silly family disputes only. Avoid private, serious, sensitive, or harmful situations.
