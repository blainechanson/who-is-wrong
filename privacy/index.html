Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Who Is Wrong is a parody entertainment website. It lets visitors submit two sides of a low-stakes argument and receive a funny AI-generated judgement.

Information You Enter

When you submit a case, the names and arguments you enter are sent to an AI service so a judgement can be generated. Do not enter private, sensitive, identifying, defamatory, bullying, unlawful, or harmful information.

Information Used For Safety

To protect the website from abuse and unexpected costs, we use rate limiting. This may involve processing technical information such as your IP address. IP addresses are hashed for rate-limit checks and are not shown publicly on the website.

Analytics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics tools, including Vercel Web Analytics, to understand page views, traffic sources, device types, and basic site performance. This helps improve the website.

Case Storage

The current version does not intentionally publish or permanently save your judgement as a public case page. A case number is created for entertainment purposes.

Third-Party Services

The website may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, rate limiting, and AI-generated responses. These services process information as needed to operate the website.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact admin@whoiswrong.ai.

Disclaimer: This website is for fun, parody, and entertainment only. AI-generated judgements are not legal advice, professional advice, mediation, counselling, or statements of fact.
