FAQ

Is this real legal advice?

No. Who Is Wrong is for parody, comedy, and entertainment only. It is not legal advice, mediation, counselling, or a serious finding of fact.

Can I use it for relationship arguments?

Yes, if the argument is harmless and low-stakes. It is best for funny couple debates, roommate disputes, family squabbles, friend drama, and workplace nonsense.

Can I share my verdict?

Yes. After the judgement appears, use the Share This Case button or copy the text manually into a message, email, or social post.

Is my judgement saved?

No. The current version creates a case number for fun, but it does not permanently save or publish your judgement.

What should I avoid entering?

Do not enter private, sensitive, identifying, defamatory, unlawful, or harmful information about yourself or anyone else.
