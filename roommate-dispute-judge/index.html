Roommate Dispute Judge

Shared living creates tiny courtroom emergencies: dishes, crumbs, laundry piles, borrowed snacks, and mysterious fridge behaviour.

Who Is Wrong gives roommate disputes a funny parody verdict without turning the kitchen into a formal tribunal.

Common Roommate Cases

  • Who left one spoon in the sink and called the kitchen clean?
  • Is it theft to eat someone else's snacks if the packet was already open?
  • Who is responsible for the last roll of toilet paper?
  • Is "I was about to do it" a valid defence after three days?

Keep It Playful

This is for harmless housemate arguments only. Serious rental, safety, harassment, or legal matters need real-world help, not a parody judge.
