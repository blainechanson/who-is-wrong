Relationship Argument Judge

Some couple debates do not need counselling. They need a pretend robe, a wooden gavel, and a completely unofficial verdict.

Who Is Wrong is made for harmless relationship arguments like dinner indecision, TV betrayal, chore debates, texting tone, and who definitely started the nonsense.

Example Couple Arguments

  • Is it wrong to watch the next episode without the person you started the show with?
  • Who is at fault when "I do not mind" somehow means "reject every dinner option"?
  • Is folding laundry optional if the clothes are technically clean?
  • Who controls the thermostat when one person is freezing and the other is dramatic?

Keep It Harmless

This page is for silly, low-stakes couple debates only. Do not use it for serious relationship, safety, legal, or personal issues.
