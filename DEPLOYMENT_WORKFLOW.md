# Who Is Wrong Deployment Workflow

After every correction or code/content change to Who Is Wrong:

1. Save the change in the project.
2. Push the updated project to GitHub.
3. Go to Vercel deployments:
   https://vercel.com/blainechansons-projects/who-is-wrong/deployments
4. Use the top deployment row.
5. Open the three-dot menu.
6. Choose redeploy.
7. Confirm the redeploy reaches Ready.
8. Tell Blaine the fresh production deployment is ready to test.

This project should not be considered finished after a change until the Vercel redeploy is done.
